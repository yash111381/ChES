Initiated in the year 2014, Chemical Engineering Society (ChES) of SVNIT, Surat under the affiliation of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE) has had a remarkable journey as a student chapter. It is the first and the only student chapter dedicated to the Chemical Engineering Dept in SVNIT. We take pride in being one of the only two student chapters under AIChE across the nation.
Over the period of only two years we have successfully completed a plethora of events, garnering a massive footfall of participants in all the events. Events like General Science Quiz and Workshops on Chem-rocket and COMSOL simulation software conducted by ChES nurtured the enthusiasm of SVNITians for learning new techniques and applying their engineering knowledge by promoting innovation in thinking and designing.
ChES believes in maximum sharing of information about various opportunities, tips and tricks, latest technologies and more. To achieve that, we have been conducting seminars on research internship opportunities and placements on and off the campus. We also conduct educational trips to various organisations of national importance. ChES has much more exciting things to offer to SVNITians through its perpetual zeal and vigour with which its members work to create an ever enriching environment in order to continue garnering support and appreciation from the students.
